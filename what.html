What We Do

We research and implement cutting-edge VR and AR solutions.

In our laboratories, we create prototypes of devices that allow us to remain at

IT

We use solutions for streaming high-definition video to the user's VR headset with minimal latency for maximal realism.

We are constantly testing and checking new products in the field of computer vision, with an emphasis on working with video.

Ourpriority is to archieve image quality that is indistinguishable from reality.

The team designs VR user interfaces for navigation and object manipulation using either controllers or hand gestures.

We are studying ways to track the body of an interlocutor so that we can use this information for the purposes of augmented reality. Another area of focus is working with sound. Sound is tremendously important for creating a unique atmosphere to be able to teleport anyone into virtual space. .

Our main interests and competencies are VR cameras, computer vision, audio, graphics, brain-computer interfaces, haptic interaction, full-body tracking, perception science, and true telepresence

Cameras

One important area of work for the company is the development of our own cameras.

We are always looking for the best technology solutions on the global market to design and manufacture cameras that meet the highest video broadcasting standards.
